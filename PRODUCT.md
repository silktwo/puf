# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

PUF speaks to military operators, procurement teams, public-sector decision makers, investors, engineering recruits, integrators, suppliers, and international partners. Visitors need to understand that the company builds serious unmanned systems without being pushed into a sales-heavy or theatrical defense narrative.

## Product Purpose

The site establishes /PUF as a drone and miltech development company: precise, capable, operationally grounded, and ready for legal procurement. Success means a visitor understands who PUF is in five seconds, sees why the company is safe to work with in the next fifteen seconds, and can route their request into catalog, pricing, support, procurement, or partnership paths.

## Brand Personality

Controlled, mechanical, sober. The brand should feel like an engineering floor before a field test: quiet, exacting, and alert.

## Anti-references

Avoid glossy sci-fi, battlefield imagery, patriotic spectacle, cyber-neon palettes, startup SaaS gradients, decorative glass cards, and lazy monospace shorthand for technical credibility. Do not make the site feel like a weapons catalog, a public price list, a full-spec catalog, or a generic AI landing page.

## Design Principles

Show engineering confidence through structure, not noise.

Let product categories carry the narrative before long explanations.

Keep motion restrained and purposeful, with text appearing in clean sequences.

Use monochrome as the system and yellow as a measured signal.

Keep contact routes visible without turning the page into a lead form funnel.

Treat the catalog as a constructor: product line first, then frame, sensor, link, and mission fit.

Separate support, procurement, and partnership requests at the first step.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-accessible controls, responsive layouts, and reduced-motion support. Copy should be available in Ukrainian and English from the first release, concise, and legible for both technical and non-technical audiences.
